About the Company

Megaworld Corporation

Megaworld Corporation, founded by Dr. Andrew Tan on August 24, 1989, is a prominent real estate company in the Philippines. Initially, Megaworld gained recognition for developing high-end residential condominiums and commercial properties in strategic urban locations across Metro Manila. In 1996, the company shifted its focus towards mixed-use community development, addressing the growing demand for integrated living, working, and leisure spaces. This strategic pivot is exemplified by projects like Eastwood City, which cater to the middle-income demographic. Beyond real estate development, Megaworld engages in project design, construction supervision, and property management, ensuring comprehensive service delivery. A significant milestone in its corporate journey occurred on August 19, 1999, when Megaworld rebranded to reflect its evolution into a holding company while maintaining its commitment to real estate excellence. Over the past 33 years, Megaworld has solidified its position as the foremost developer of residential and office spaces in the Philippines, consistently adapting to changing market dynamics. Dr. Andrew L. Tan, the visionary founder and Chairman, pioneered the Live-Work-Play-Learn township concept. This innovative approach integrates residential buildings, office towers, commercial hubs, and luxury accommodations within vibrant communities, setting Megaworld apart and underscoring its dedication to creating holistic urban environments that cater to diverse lifestyle needs.
